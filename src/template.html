About me


Why I chose Design?
Design always fascinated me because, I feel it is a medium through which one can enhance the communication between the physical world and the material world.
For me design is like bending a metal or folding a sheet of paper in such a way, that the product becomes more efficient, cutting edge, aesthetically pleasant and simple to use.
Apart from that I love observing things, appreciate nature, learn from the ancient art culture, Web Development and sometimes Photography.
